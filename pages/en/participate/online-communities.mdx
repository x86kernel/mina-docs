# Online Communities
Learn all about Mina’s online communities

Mina has many online communities to connect with other Mina enthusiasts, builders, node operators, developers, mentors, and experts. Come join us!

### Main Community
The largest group of Mina technical users can be found on Discord — we recommend starting here and joining the channels that suit your interests:

[<img src="/static/img/docs-images/Discord.png" alt="Terminal screenshot of Mina’s zkApps from a command-line interface." width="2.5%"/> &nbsp;Official Discord Server for Mina Protocol](https://discord.com/invite/RDQc43H)
